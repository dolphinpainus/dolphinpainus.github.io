Donations

If you'd like to donate to help us maintain this site, and do other fun things to make everyone's experience better at Insomniac Gamers, we'd greatly appreciate it. Donations aren't mandatory in any way, and they all go into keeping this site alive, hosting contests, and other fun things for you guys. We are completely non-profit, making no money from anything we do here. We do it because it's fun for us, and we hope it's fun for you also. If you have suggestions on what you'd like to see the donations go into, suggest it on the forums or through email.

If you donate and would like to be added to our list of donators, email us your chatango name and info so we can add you! You can also a note on paypal letting us know.

If your donation is going towards Minecraft, be sure to include your Minecraft name in your note letting us know!

Thanks again and we appreciate every donation.
If you've already donated in the past but don't see your name on the list, email letting us know so we can add you! Or if you'd like to be removed from the list just let us know.

Email to contact: IGSiteManagers@Insomniacgamers.net
