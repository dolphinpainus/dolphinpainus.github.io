www.InsomniacGamers.net
Insomniac Gamers is a website dedicated to 24/7 streaming of games. We stream a variety of games, anything from classics to new releases from any and every console. On our forums you may make requests for games you may like to see, or even participate in different contests for prizes! If you decide to stay around for awhile, introduce yourself to us on the forums. Thank you, and we hope you enjoy your experience here at Insomniac Gamers.
